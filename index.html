The Opportunity in BC

Thousands of BC Businesses
Will Change Ownership

Across British Columbia, thousands of small and mid-sized businesses will change ownership in the coming decade — yet many owners are not prepared for this critical transition. Successful transactions require professional guidance, careful preparation, and access to qualified buyers and sellers.

01

Approaching Retirement

Many founders are ready to step back but haven't begun planning their exit. Without a clear strategy, years of built-up value can erode rapidly during an unplanned transition.

02

Unsure of Value

Most owners don't know what their business is truly worth in today's market. Overestimating or underestimating costs money at every stage of a transaction.

04

Preparing for a Successful Exit

The Most Valuable Sales Are
Planned Well in Advance

Owners who prepare thoroughly consistently achieve better outcomes — higher valuations, smoother transactions, and fewer surprises at closing.

💰 Financial Readiness

  • Clean, well-organized financial statements for at least 3 years
  • Consistent revenue and demonstrated profitability
  • Clear separation of owner personal and business expenses
  • Tax compliance fully up to date

⚙️ Operational Stability

  • Documented systems and standard operating procedures
  • Reduced owner dependency — business runs without you
  • Key staff retained with appropriate agreements in place
  • Supplier and customer relationships documented

📋 Legal & Structural Readiness

  • Corporate records, contracts, and licences organized
  • Proper documentation in place and fully up to date
  • Lease agreements reviewed and assignable where possible
  • IP, trademarks, and domains properly registered

📈 Market Positioning

  • Clear and credible growth potential articulated
  • Strong customer relationships and demonstrable retention
  • Online presence and reputation actively managed
  • Competitive advantages clearly defined and defensible
